Delivery APP

1. Auth cycle:-

The DELIVERY MAN sign in just. And he's signup or created in the admin dashboard.

2. Status Cycle :-

The delivery man always started with offline status (even it close the app or just made it unActive or pausing it with all these cases it will be offline) . So he should be online to receive new order.

3. Order Items :-

Seeing order that have been sent to the delivery man and he have to accept it

4- Map screen :-

Tracking the user and rest coordinates and your coordinates also with live stream updating data

5- Order Screen :-

Change status to delivered to ensure that you delivered that order and return the cycle again from order item screen.

6- Profile :-

Showing the profile data and every thing about the delivery man.
